Movement is the answer

Find rhythm in motion, not in timing.

Concept

Driftform is built around a single idea:
here's the weird part - the puzzle literally builds itself as stuff moves around.

There are no static puzzles. Every level exists only while something is moving. Stop, and the space reshapes itself.

Interaction Rules

You get good at it by learning how the game reacts to what you do.

  • One input controls motion bias, not exact direction

  • Acceleration matters more than position

  • Collisions can reshape the environment

  • Repeated movement patterns unlock new behaviors

Visual Language

We basically stripped out all the UI clutter because we didn't want anything messing with your flow state.

  • High contrast dark backgrounds

  • Neon accents that react to motion

  • Long exposure-style trails

  • Minimal UI elements
